FAQ

Find answers to some of the most common questions regarding services, project processes, and collaboration.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Not only do I offer the expected maintenance and security but I also offer design and logo support. My goal is to become and extension of your in-house marketing team and I will tailor a team and empowers your internal team.

Do you work with a small business?

I am passionate about collaborating with business of all sizes, including small enterprises. I understand the unique challenges and aspirations of small businesses and tailor our services to meet their specific needs. My goal is to empower small businesses with memorable logos and impactful branding solutions that help you stand out in the competitive market. Whether you're just starting or looking to refresh your brand, I am here to elevate your small business presence with creativity and strategic design.

How long does the project take on average?

The time it takes to complete a project depends on its complexity. For a basic website, it typically takes 2 to 4 weeks. More complex projects, like e-commerce platforms or custom web applications, may take 6 to 12 weeks or longer. This includes design, development, testing, and revisions to ensure everything meets your expectations. I work closely with clients to meet deadlines while maintaining a high standard of quality.

Is there a possibility of offline meetings?

Yes, offline meetings can be arranged based on your preference and location. I am flexible with communication methods and can schedule in-person meetings to discuss project details, progress, or feedback. However, for clients in distant locations, I usually recommend virtual meetings as a more efficient option to ensure regular updates and faster collaboration.
